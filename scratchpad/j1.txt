feat(journal): record pilot actions to a persistent ship's log

Add an append-only `events` table (SQLite) holding the full action history for
long-term stats, plus a LogEvent type; the most recent JOURNAL_WINDOW (1000)
load into AppState.journal at boot. Actions stage a pre-rendered captain's-log
line into a pending_journal buffer (mirroring pending_fire), drained by the
event loop to persist and prepend to the journal. First set of captured
actions: travel, mine, deploy waypoint, drop/detach container, storage move.
